Your Guide to Making the Best Choices

As a true Libra ⚖️, I wanted to keep things democratic and ensure everyone feels included. That's why I've divided the day into two parts, so whether you're more outgoing 💃🏽 🕺🏽 or prefer a cozy vibe 🥰, there's a moment for you to enjoy and celebrate with me!

(Kind of a) Chill Moment 🥳

Starting at 9 PM, join us at Cabal for a fun yet laid-back celebration with a DJ — dancing is optional, of course, although very welcome! We'll have a reserved table for good conversations and delicious drinks in a relaxed vibe. If the weather's nice ☀️, we'll be sitting outside, so don't forget to bring a jacket. Cabal is known for its great cocktails 🍸 and small bites, so I recommend coming with a full belly, but leave some room for the delicious pistachio and raspberry cake 🎂 that Matuta will make for us! The place stays open until 1:30 or 2 AM, giving us plenty of time to enjoy the night together.

Partying Hard Vibes

Only the strong will make it to Outra Cena! Unfortunately, The club only opens at 1 AM, so don't feel bad if you're not joining us. But for those who do, get ready for one of the best nights of your life 👯‍♀️. Located in Marvila, Outra Cena is considered Lisbon's top electronic music nightclub 🎶, even recognized by Resident Advisor. Housed in a century-old former winery, the venue features futuristic light design and a powerful sound system across two dance floors. Open until 8 AM, the club is a tribute to underground culture, with a strict no photos/videos policy for an immersive experience.
